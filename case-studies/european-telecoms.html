Telecommunications Compliance By Design

European HR
Integration & Compliance.

Delivering a scalable and compliant HR model for a European Telecommunications Group to manage multi-jurisdiction regulatory complexity.

01 Strategic Imperative

The European telecommunications sector operates within one of the world's most complex regulatory environments. Managing a workforce distributed across multiple jurisdictions requires a delicate balance between global visibility and local labor laws.

The Challenge

Fragmentation caused a "Compliance & Visibility" crisis. Headcount and skill data were trapped in local spreadsheets, posing severe GDPR risks regarding data minimisation and the "Right to be Forgotten."

The Objective

Establish a scalable integration model with Workday as the central "System of Record," ensuring interoperability with local payroll while maintaining 100% regulatory sovereignty.

02 Solution Architecture

To address the diversity of the European landscape, we architected a **"Hub and Spoke"** integration strategy, ensuring consistency while respecting local deviations.

Custom API Layer for Sovereignty

Built a privacy layer using "Privacy by Design" principles. Custom logic ensures sensitive data for regions like Germany is masked before transmission to non-EU sub-processors.

Workday Studio & IAM

Leveraged Studio to build sophisticated Identity and Access Management (IAM) flows. Access to critical network infrastructure is now provisioned or revoked in real-time based on HR events.

Bulk Operations via EIB

Utilised Enterprise Interface Builders for high-volume monthly benefits and bonus data transfers, enabling secure batch processing for thousands of records.

Agile Delivery & Works Council Engagement

Success was as much cultural as technical. By adopting 2-week sprints and demonstrating security features early via sprint demos, we secured vital buy-in from local Works Councils in Germany and France.
